Understanding the Site & Data

Voice Tags (e.g., ðŸŒ¿ Calm, ðŸ”¥ Intense)

These tags attempt to capture the speaker's vocal delivery style during the testimonial. They are derived automatically from analyzing the audio's tone, speed, and emotional cues, sometimes refined manually. They add an extra layer to help you connect with the feeling behind the story.

Sentiment Scores

You might see references to sentiment scores in the underlying data description. These were calculated using Natural Language Processing (NLP) models to get a general sense of the positivity (overall_mean_sentiment) and hopefulness (sentiment_hope_score) expressed in the testimonial transcripts.

Data Cleaning & Normalization

Conditions and methods mentioned in testimonials can be described in many ways. Efforts were made to group similar terms (like "hypothyroidism" and "thyroid issues" into "thyroid condition") using automated techniques (fuzzy matching) to make filtering more effective.

Healing Insights

The insights shown are derived from analyzing trends and specific statements across multiple testimonials in the dataset, combined with relevant quotes and suggested actions.
